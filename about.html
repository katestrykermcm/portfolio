Hi, I'm Kate!

I’m passionate about projects at the intersection of creative and technical fields. I design information, interactions, and experiences. I write code and rapidly iterate to bring them to life. I love working with people, from collaborating with my team to understanding our user's needs.

After graduating from Colorado College with a computer science degree, I created information visualizations and designed/developed web applications for a team of tech security experts in NYC. Following, I returned to school at Carnegie Mellon University, where I recently completed a masters in Human-Computer Interaction.
