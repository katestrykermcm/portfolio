Overview

Cyber security is a constant challenge for all modern companies. Although it is impossible to ever be completely impervious to an attack, Chief Information Security Officers can intelligently allocate their resources to substantially fortify their methods of protection. Like doctors, when making decisions, CISOs pair their deep expertise with relevant and current information. The CISO Dashboard swiftly and competently “takes the temperature” of the company's network. It equips CISOs with meaningful data helpful in assessing the “health” of their network, specifically identifying the threats and risks they face. The CISO Dashboard does not aim to be a constant monitoring tool or to provide easy answers, but rather to empower CISOs to make informed decisions.

Providing Deeper Content

For each project, I would meet with the specialized team of experts to be briefed on the case and their efforts. The creation process was typically extremely iterative; I would constantly interface with the specialized experts to update and improve my work based on their findings, while getting their feedback to ensure clarity and accuracy.
